Something to introduce about myself.

My name is Nick Vu, a down-to-earth electrical engineer. I love playing guitar, designing things and reading books.

Besides my professional skills in electrical engineering, I have a very very ardent interest in Immersive Virtual Reality. Not similar to the haptic feedback sensations VR out there, but rather the more neuroscience related implementation. I want to make a machine that can be controlled intuitively and experienced naturally, through the usage of digital signal processing and electrophysiology. If you have time to breeze through my website, most of my experience and projects are deeply related to reach closer to my life goal.
